About Us

Hello! Southern Art Soap was established in 2019 by Tatiana Churzin, a soap-making hobbyist who lives in the beautiful green state of North Carolina. Her other favorite hobby is baking delicious desserts.

We offer an enormous variety of shapes, colors, fragrances, and designs and are happy to work with you if you need something more specific. Please send me a message if you would like to ask any questions.
